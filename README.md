##  About Me

I build backend systems where logic, data, and structure matter more than surface polish.

My background is rooted in **C#/.NET development**, APIs, and database-driven systems, combined with hands-on experience in **systems administration**. That mix made me opinionated about clean code, explicit design, and understanding whats actually happening under the hood.

I enjoy working on problems where correctness matters, abstractions are earned, and complexity is intentional. When something breaks, I want to know *why* - not just how to patch it.
